I worked on Henchmen and soon to be released The Willoughbys. I’ve also worked as Lighting TD for MPC Film in Montréal, Québec where I worked on Marvel’s Fantastic Four, Disney’s The Finest Hours and Warner Bros’ King Arthur: Legend of the Sword.
